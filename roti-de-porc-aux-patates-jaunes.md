## Ingredients

- 1 pork shoulder roast (2 kg/4.4 lb) with bone and skin
- 2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 55 g (1/4 cup) butter
- 2 onions, minced
- 15 ml (1 tablespoon) dry mustard
- 250 ml (1 cup) chicken broth or water
- 8 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and halved or quartered

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 165°C (325°F) with rack in center.
2. Make small incisions in the roast and insert sliced garlic.
3. Brown the roast in butter in a large enameled cast iron pot, then season with salt and pepper.
4. Brown onions with mustard in the same pot. Season, add broth, return roast to pot, cover and bake for 3.5 hours until meat separates easily from bone.
5. Transfer roast to plate and tent with foil. Bring cooking broth and potatoes to boil in pot, cover and simmer 30 minutes until potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally.
6. Serve roast pieces with potatoes.
