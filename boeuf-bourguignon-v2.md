## Ingredients

- 40 g (3 tbsp) softened butter
- 30 g (3 tbsp) all-purpose flour
- 1 kg (2.2 lb) sirloin tip beef, cubed
- 45 ml (3 tbsp) olive oil
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 500 ml (2 cups) light red wine
- 500 ml (2 cups) beef broth
- 1 French shallot, peeled
- 1 clove
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 carrots, peeled and diced
- 115 g (1/4 lb) bacon slices, cut into lardons
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) olive oil (for garnish)
- 450 g (1 lb) white mushrooms, halved or quartered
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped (for garnish)
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) fresh parsley, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) with rack in center.
2. Mix softened butter with flour and set aside.
3. Brown half the beef cubes in oil, season and set aside. Repeat with remaining meat.
4. In same pot, brown onion. Add garlic (1 min), deglaze with wine (5 min simmer), add broth and butter mixture and bring to boil while whisking. Add meat, shallot with clove, and bay leaf. Season and cover.
5. Bake 1.5 hours, add carrots and continue covered 30-45 minutes until meat is fork-tender. Remove shallot, clove, and bay leaf.

### For the garnish:
1. Brown lardons in oil, add mushrooms until golden, add garlic and parsley (1 min).
2. Add to stew and adjust seasoning.

## Notes

Freezer-friendly: Cool slightly and freeze in airtight containers or freezer bags laid flat. Stack after freezing.
