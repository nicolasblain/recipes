## Ingredients

- 200g full-fat plain Greek yogurt (unsweetened)
- 30g walnuts, roughly broken
- 60g fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries)

## Instructions

1. Spoon the yogurt into a bowl.
2. Top with walnuts and berries.
3. Eat immediately.

## Notes

- Full-fat, unsweetened only. Low-fat yogurt adds sugar to compensate for flavour, and flavoured yogurts are often very high in sugar.
- Berries are the lowest-carb fruit option. Avoid banana, mango, or grapes here.
- Walnuts add fat and protein that slow digestion and keep you full longer.
