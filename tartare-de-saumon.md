## Ingredients

- 675 g (1 1/2 lb) fresh salmon
- 20 ml (4 tsp) finely chopped French shallots
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) white wine vinegar
- 15 ml (1 tbsp) water
- 45 ml (3 tbsp) olive oil
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) chopped chives
- 20 ml (4 tsp) lemon juice
- 15 ml (1 tbsp) finely chopped flat parsley
- 15 ml (1 tbsp) old-fashioned mustard
- 15 ml (1 tbsp) chopped capers
- 5 ml (1 tsp) Tabasco sauce (or to taste)
- Ice cubes

## Instructions

1. Fill a bowl two-thirds with ice and place a second bowl on top to keep the fish cold during preparation.
2. Cut salmon into small cubes on a work surface, placing them in the chilled bowl as you work. Refrigerate.
3. Mix shallots, vinegar, and water in a small bowl. Let macerate 5 minutes, then drain and discard liquid.
4. Add shallots and remaining ingredients to the salmon. Season with salt and pepper and mix well.
5. Serve immediately with green salad, croutons, or plain chips.
