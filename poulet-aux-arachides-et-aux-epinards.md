## Ingredients

- 250 ml peanut butter
- 15 ml Thai sauce
- 15 ml honey
- 15 ml sesame oil
- 15 ml soy sauce
- 15 ml sugar
- Sriracha to taste
- 375 ml warm water
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
- 30 ml butter or oil
- 375 ml baby spinach
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 125 ml roasted salted peanuts, crushed
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk together peanut butter, Thai sauce, honey, sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar, and sriracha until combined. Gradually add warm water while whisking until smooth.

2. Brown chicken strips in butter, season with salt and pepper.

3. Add sauce to chicken along with spinach and carrot. Bring to a boil, then simmer over medium heat until carrot is tender and sauce reduces by half.

4. Serve over udon noodles or rice, garnished with crushed peanuts.
