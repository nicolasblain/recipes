## Ingredients

- 2 beets, peeled and cut into 12 wedges each
- 1 small butternut squash, peeled and cubed
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- 450 g small nantes carrots, halved lengthwise
- 30 ml olive oil
- 205 g pearl barley
- 30 ml olive oil
- 90 g dates, pitted and diced
- 2.5 ml garam masala
- 15 ml lemon juice
- 125 ml plain Greek yogurt
- 1 small green apple, julienned
- 25 g roasted pecans, crushed
- Fresh basil leaves, to taste
- Lemon wedges, to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Arrange beets, squash, onion, and carrots on the sheet; drizzle with oil, season with salt and pepper. Roast for 45 minutes until tender and lightly golden.

2. Meanwhile, boil pearl barley in salted water for 35 minutes until tender. Drain and rinse under cold water.

3. Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté the barley for 2 minutes. Add dates and garam masala; cook for 2 minutes. Add lemon juice and season to taste.

4. Distribute barley and roasted vegetables among serving bowls. Top each with Greek yogurt, julienned apple, crushed pecans, and fresh basil. Serve with lemon wedges.
