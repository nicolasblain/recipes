## Ingredients

- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped (seeds removed or kept)
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) butter
- 1 kg (2.2 lb) ground beef
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) chili seasoning
- 5 ml (1 tsp) ground cumin
- 1 can (796 ml/28 oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (540 ml/19 oz) red beans, rinsed and drained
- 250 ml (1 cup) brewed coffee or strong coffee
- 60 ml (1/4 cup) beef broth or water
- 60 ml (1/4 cup) ketchup
- 45 ml (3 tbsp) lime juice
- 10 g (1/4 cup) fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

## Instructions

1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, brown the onion and pepper in butter. Add beef and cook, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon until lightly browned. Season with salt and pepper. Add garlic and spices and cook 2 minutes while stirring.
2. Add remaining ingredients except cilantro. Bring to boil and simmer partially covered for 45 minutes until thickened, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasoning and add cilantro if desired.

## Notes

Freezer-friendly recipe.
