## Ingredients

- 60 ml reduced-sodium soy sauce
- 30 ml brown sugar
- 30 ml toasted sesame oil
- 5 ml sambal oelek
- 5 ml fresh ginger, finely grated
- 1 lime, zest and juice
- 450 g medium shrimp (20-30 count), peeled with tail intact
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 225 g rice vermicelli
- 180 g shredded iceberg lettuce
- 2 Lebanese cucumbers, cut into half-moons
- 10 g fresh mint leaves
- 1 lime, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, sambal oelek, ginger, and lime zest and juice in a large bowl. Set aside.

2. Place a barbecue wok on the grill grate and preheat to high heat.

3. Toss shrimp and onion with 45 ml of the dressing. Grill in the wok for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cooked through.

4. Boil rice vermicelli for 3 minutes until tender. Drain and rinse under cold water.

5. Mix the cooked noodles with the remaining dressing, lettuce, cucumbers, and half the mint.

6. Plate the noodle salad and top with the grilled shrimp mixture. Garnish with remaining mint and serve with lime wedges.
