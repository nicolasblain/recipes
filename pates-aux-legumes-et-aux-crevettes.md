## Ingredients

- 3 multicolored bell peppers, seeded and cubed
- 2 onions, sliced into thin quarters
- 227 g white mushrooms, sliced
- 30 ml olive oil
- 340 g medium asparagus, trimmed and halved
- 115 g fusilli or penne pasta
- 340 g raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 ml red pepper flakes
- 6 green onions, finely chopped
- 60 ml chicken broth
- 1 lemon, zest finely grated
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 15 ml tomato paste
- 60 ml fresh basil leaves
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. Toss peppers, onions, and mushrooms with half the olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and roast for 25 minutes, stirring halfway. Add asparagus and cook for another 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.

3. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain and lightly oil.

4. In a skillet, cook shrimp with garlic and red pepper flakes in remaining oil until done. Add green onions for 1 minute, then broth, lemon zest, juice, and tomato paste. Bring to a boil, then add pasta and half the roasted vegetables. Toss and adjust seasoning.

5. Divide pasta among bowls, top with remaining vegetables, and garnish with fresh basil.
