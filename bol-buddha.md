## Ingredients

- 75 ml mayonnaise
- 30 ml peanut butter
- 30 ml water
- 15 ml rice vinegar
- 5 ml sambal oelek
- 5 ml soy sauce
- 15 ml rice vinegar
- 2.5 ml sugar
- 1 pinch crushed chili flakes
- 170 g shredded cabbage
- 1 carrot, cut into thin ribbons
- 1 can (540 ml) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 15 ml vegetable oil
- 225 g green beans, trimmed and cut diagonally in half
- 225 g soba noodles
- 1 small mango, peeled and diced
- 40 g roasted salted peanuts, crushed
- 30 ml fresh cilantro, chopped

## Instructions

1. Whisk together mayonnaise, peanut butter, water, 15 ml vinegar, sambal oelek, and soy sauce until smooth. Season and set aside.

2. Combine 15 ml vinegar, sugar, and chili flakes until sugar dissolves. Add cabbage and carrot; season, mix, and let marinate for 15 minutes.

3. Brown the chickpeas in oil over medium heat in a non-stick pan. Season and set aside.

4. Cook green beans for 4 minutes in boiling salted water until tender-crisp, then ice-bath for 2 minutes. In the same pot, cook soba noodles al dente, drain, and lightly oil.

5. Distribute noodles among bowls. Top with marinated vegetables, chickpeas, and green beans. Garnish with mango, peanuts, and cilantro. Drizzle with peanut sauce.
