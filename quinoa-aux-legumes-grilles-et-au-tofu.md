## Ingredients

- 450 g firm tofu, diced
- 500 ml water
- 10 ml salt
- 250 ml water
- 225 g quinoa, rinsed and drained
- 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and cubed
- 45 ml olive oil
- 2 zucchini, halved lengthwise and sliced
- 1 can (398 ml) artichoke hearts, rinsed and drained
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tomato, thinly sliced
- 10 g flat parsley leaves, torn
- 1 lemon, zest and juice
- 5 ml dried oregano

## Instructions

1. Place diced tofu in a heat-resistant bowl.

2. Bring 500 ml water and salt to a boil; pour over tofu and let marinate while preparing the salad.

3. Boil 250 ml water with the quinoa, salt, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until al dente. Rest for 5 minutes and fluff with a fork.

4. Sauté the yellow pepper in 30 ml oil for 2 minutes. Add zucchini and cook for 3 minutes until golden. Transfer to a large bowl.

5. Drain the tofu and discard the liquid.

6. Brown the artichoke hearts in the remaining oil for 1 minute. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute more. Transfer to the bowl.

7. Combine the tofu, cooked quinoa, tomato, parsley, lemon zest and juice, and oregano with the vegetables. Season to taste.
