## Ingredients

- 30 g nutritional yeast flakes
- 60 ml olive oil
- 30 ml tamari
- 30 ml water
- 15 ml maple syrup
- 1 garlic clove, halved

## Instructions

1. Using a personal blender with grinding blade or a hand mixer in a bowl, blend all ingredients into a smooth puree.

2. Transfer to an airtight container. The sauce keeps refrigerated for 4 days.

## Notes

This vegan condiment is inspired by the popular recipe from Montreal restaurant Aux Vivres. The combination of tamari and nutritional yeast creates a signature umami flavor.
