## Ingredients

### Hollandaise Sauce:
- 45 ml water
- 15 ml white wine vinegar
- 3 egg yolks
- 180 ml unsalted butter, melted
- Salt and pepper

### Poached Eggs:
- 15 ml white vinegar
- 8 eggs
- Salt

### Toppings:
- 8 slices back bacon or smoked ham
- 30 ml butter
- 5 ml maple syrup
- 4 English muffins, halved
- 4 slices crispy bacon, finely sliced
- 15 ml fresh chives, chopped
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

### Hollandaise Sauce:
1. Whisk water, white wine vinegar, and egg yolks in a double boiler bowl off heat.
2. Place over simmering water and whisk until thick and foamy, avoiding overcooking.
3. Remove from heat. Drizzle in melted butter while whisking. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and keep warm.

### Poached Eggs:
Add vinegar to simmering salted water. Crack eggs into small ramekins and slide into water gradually, cooking 4 at a time for 2-3 minutes until whites are set but yolks remain runny. Drain on paper towels.

### Assembly:
1. Heat back bacon in butter and maple syrup for about 1 minute.
2. Toast English muffins.
3. Spread hollandaise on muffins, add bacon, place one egg per half-muffin. Top with more sauce, bacon, and chives.
