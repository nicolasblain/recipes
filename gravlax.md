## Ingredients

- 125 ml (1/2 cup) coarse salt
- 125 ml (1/2 cup) sugar
- 2 salmon fillets with skin, approximately 800 g (1 3/4 lb) each
- 1 large bunch fresh dill
- Fresh dill sprigs for garnish
- Freshly ground pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine salt and sugar in a bowl.
2. Spread half the salt mixture on one fillet, cover generously with dill, add remaining salt mixture, and top with the second fillet (flesh-side down).
3. Wrap fillets tightly in aluminum foil, place on a baking sheet, cover with another sheet and weight down with a heavy object.
4. Refrigerate 36-48 hours, turning salmon every 12 hours and discarding accumulated liquid as needed.
5. Unwrap fillets, discard dill, and rinse thoroughly under cold running water. Pat dry completely.
6. Using a sharp knife, slice fillets thinly starting from the thickest end.
7. Serve garnished with fresh dill and pepper, accompanied by mustard hollandaise sauce and pickled capers.

## Notes

Total maceration time: 36-48 hours (minimum 2 days).
