## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Chicken thighs
- Turnips
- Carrots
- Green cabbage
- Zucchini
- Merguez sausages
- Chickpeas
- Tomatoes
- Saffron
- Ras-el-hanout
- Couscous grains
- Raisins
- Butter
- Broth
- Onion
- Garlic
- White wine
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Brown the lamb and chicken in olive oil and butter, then sauté onion with garlic and saffron. Deglaze with white wine and return the meat to the pot.

2. Add vegetables and spices, then simmer covered for 35 minutes.

3. Add sausages and remaining vegetables, continuing to cook for another 10 minutes until tender.

4. For the couscous: Bring broth and butter to a boil, remove from heat, stir in couscous and raisins, cover and let sit for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.

5. Distribute couscous on a platter and top with the meat, vegetable mixture, and broth. Allow guests to serve themselves.
