## Ingredients

- 30 ml lemon juice
- 30 ml Dijon mustard
- 15 ml whole-grain mustard
- 1 egg yolk
- 15 ml chopped capers
- 10 ml sambal oelek
- 60 ml olive oil
- 675 g fresh beef tenderloin (not tenderized)
- 30 ml flat parsley, minced
- 45 ml chives, minced
- 45 ml shallots, finely minced

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, mustards, egg yolk, capers, and sambal oelek. Gradually whisk in oil while whisking continuously. Adjust seasoning.
2. Finely chop the beef using a knife. Add dressing, herbs, and shallots to the mixture. Season with salt and pepper and mix well. Add additional sambal oelek if desired.
3. Serve with toasted baguette croutons, homemade fries, and green salad.
