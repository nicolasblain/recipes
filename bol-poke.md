## Ingredients

- 320 g Calrose rice (sushi rice)
- 430 ml water
- 2.5 ml salt
- 30 ml soy sauce
- 10 ml toasted sesame oil
- 10 ml rice vinegar
- 340 g fresh tuna or salmon, diced
- 10 ml black sesame seeds
- 125 ml mayonnaise
- 30 ml water
- 10 ml sriracha sauce
- 210 g blanched frozen edamame
- 2 Lebanese cucumbers, thinly sliced
- 2 carrots, julienned
- 1 avocado, diced
- 10 g cilantro leaves
- Lime wedges for serving

## Instructions

1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Boil rice with water and salt, then simmer covered for 15 minutes. Rest for 10 minutes covered, then fluff with a fork.

2. Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, and vinegar. Add fish and sesame seeds, then marinate for 15 minutes.

3. Whisk together mayonnaise, water, and sriracha until smooth.

4. Distribute rice in bowls and top with marinated fish, vegetables, and cilantro. Serve with sauce and lime wedges.
