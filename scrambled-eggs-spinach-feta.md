## Ingredients

- 3 large eggs
- 1 large handful fresh spinach
- 40g feta cheese, crumbled
- 1 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and beat lightly with a fork.
2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat.
3. Add the spinach and sauté for 1–2 minutes until wilted. Push to the side.
4. Pour in the eggs. Let them set slightly on the bottom, then gently fold with a spatula, moving the curds slowly. Keep the heat low — don't rush it.
5. When the eggs are just barely set (still slightly glossy), remove from heat. They'll finish cooking from residual heat.
6. Top with crumbled feta and serve immediately.

## Notes

- Low heat and slow folding are the difference between good and bad scrambled eggs. Don't walk away from the pan.
- If you want more volume, add a 4th egg rather than increasing other ingredients.
- A pinch of chili flakes works well here.
