## Ingredients

- 375 ml beef broth
- 14 g dried porcini mushrooms
- 4 chicken thighs
- 15 ml olive oil
- 225 g white mushrooms, quartered
- 225 g oyster mushrooms, sliced
- 30 ml butter
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 30 ml all-purpose flour
- 125 ml dry white wine
- 1 small tomato, seeded and diced
- 5 ml fresh tarragon, chopped
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat broth to boiling, remove from heat, add dried mushrooms, rest for 15 minutes to rehydrate, then drain and reserve both separately.

2. Remove skin from chicken thighs and cut each in half, separating the thighs and drumsticks.

3. Brown chicken in oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat on both sides. Season with salt and pepper and reserve in a bowl. Deglaze pot with 1/4 cup broth, scraping the bottom, and pour over chicken.

4. In the same pot, brown fresh mushrooms in butter. Add rehydrated mushrooms, onion, and garlic, cooking for 2 minutes. Sprinkle flour and coat well. Deglaze with wine, reducing by half while stirring. Add remaining broth, tomato, and reserved chicken. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer for 1 hour on low heat.

5. Uncover and reduce sauce for 5 minutes until it coats the back of a spoon. Adjust seasoning, add tarragon, and serve with buttered pasta if desired.
