## Ingredients

- 225 g (1 1/2 cups) unbleached all-purpose flour
- 5 ml (1 tsp) baking powder
- 5 ml (1 tsp) baking soda
- 310 ml (1 1/4 cups) mashed ripe bananas (3-4 bananas)
- 125 ml (1/2 cup) coconut milk
- 15 ml (1 tbsp) lime juice
- 115 g (1/2 cup) unsalted softened butter
- 210 g (1 cup) sugar
- 1 egg
- 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Butter a loaf pan and line with parchment paper.
2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and baking soda and set aside.
3. In another bowl, mix bananas, coconut milk, and lime juice.
4. In a third bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla, then mix dry ingredients alternately with banana mixture. Pour into pan.
5. Bake 1 hour until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
6. Cool on rack, then unmold and slice.
