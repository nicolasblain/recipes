## Ingredients

- 1 can (160g drained) tuna in olive oil
- 2 large handfuls mixed greens or arugula
- ½ cucumber, diced
- ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 tbsp capers
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar or lemon juice
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Drain the tuna and break it into chunks in a bowl.
2. Combine with the cucumber, red onion, and capers.
3. Whisk together olive oil and vinegar (or lemon), season with salt and pepper.
4. Toss the greens with the dressing.
5. Pile the tuna mixture on top of the greens and serve.

## Notes

- Tuna in olive oil is noticeably better than tuna in water — keep a few cans stocked.
- The red onion can be soaked in cold water for 5 minutes to mellow its sharpness if you find it too strong.
- This takes 5 minutes and keeps you full for hours — a reliable default lunch.
