## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cubed
- 125 ml plain yogurt
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 15 ml fresh ginger, minced
- 15 ml garam masala
- 2.5 ml salt
- 1 lime, juiced
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 small jalapeño, seeded and chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 15 ml fresh ginger, chopped
- 40 g butter
- 15 ml garam masala
- 15 ml tomato paste
- 1 can (398 ml) diced tomatoes
- 125 ml chicken broth
- 125 ml 35% cream
- 10 g fresh cilantro, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 260°C (500°F). Line a baking sheet with foil and butter lightly. Mix chicken with yogurt, first batch of garlic, ginger, garam masala, salt, and lime juice. Spread on sheet and bake 15 minutes until cooked through.

2. Sauté onion, jalapeño, second batch of garlic, and ginger in butter. Add garam masala and tomato paste, cooking for 2 minutes.

3. Add tomatoes and broth, then simmer for 15 minutes.

4. Stir in cream and use an immersion blender to puree the sauce until smooth.

5. Add cooked chicken to sauce and simmer until thickened. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice and naan.
