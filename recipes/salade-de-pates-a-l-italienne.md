## Ingredients

- 3 green onions, finely chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 75 ml olive oil
- 8 sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained and chopped
- 115 g prosciutto (2 thick slices), diced
- 450 g farfalle or short pasta
- 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and diced
- 20 cherry tomatoes, halved
- 200 g bocconcini cheese, drained and chopped
- 12 black olives, pitted and halved
- 12 green olives, pitted and halved
- 30 ml balsamic vinegar
- 10 g fresh basil, chopped
- 15 ml fresh oregano, chopped

## Instructions

1. In a pan over medium heat, soften the green onions and garlic in 30 ml oil for 5 minutes. Add sun-dried tomatoes and prosciutto; cook for 3 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, cook the pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. Rinse under cold water and drain thoroughly.

3. Combine the cooled pasta, onion mixture, bell pepper, tomatoes, cheese, olives, remaining oil, vinegar, and herbs in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm or chilled.
