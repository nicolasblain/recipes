## Ingredients

- 90 g quinoa, rinsed and drained
- 225 g firm tofu, crumbled
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 30 ml olive oil
- 225 g kale, stems removed, thinly sliced
- 15 ml lemon juice
- 15 ml olive oil
- 265 g red grapes, halved
- 2 Lebanese cucumbers, cut into thin sticks (5 cm long)
- 1/2 red onion, finely diced
- 40 g pumpkin seeds
- Dragon sauce (see recipe)
- 4 radishes, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Cook rinsed quinoa in salted boiling water for 12 minutes until tender. Drain.

2. In a large non-stick skillet over high heat, brown the tofu and garlic in oil. Add the cooked quinoa and cook for 2 more minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

3. In a large bowl, massage the kale with lemon juice, oil, and salt for about 2 minutes to soften. Add half the grapes, cucumbers, onion, and pumpkin seeds. Adjust seasoning.

4. Plate the quinoa mixture and top with the kale salad. Drizzle with dragon sauce and garnish with remaining grapes and radishes.
