## Ingredients

- 2 large sweet potatoes, cubed
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 45 ml olive oil
- 5 ml sweet paprika
- 5 ml ground coriander
- 1 can (540 ml) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 10 ml harissa
- 250 ml plain 2% Greek yogurt
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 1 ripe avocado
- 240 g kale, thinly sliced
- 30 ml olive oil
- 140 g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 60 g crumbled feta cheese (optional)
- 1/2 small red onion, sliced
- 1 lemon, cut into wedges (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).

2. Toss sweet potatoes with garlic, 45 ml oil, paprika, and coriander on a baking sheet. Season with salt and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes until tender.

3. Mix chickpeas with harissa and season with salt and pepper.

4. Add chickpeas to the baking sheet beside potatoes. Continue roasting for 5 minutes.

5. Process Greek yogurt, lemon juice, and avocado until smooth. Season with salt.

6. Massage the kale with 30 ml oil and salt until softened.

7. Assemble bowls with sweet potatoes, chickpeas, kale, and tomatoes. Top with feta cheese, avocado-yogurt sauce, and onion. Serve with lemon wedges if desired.
