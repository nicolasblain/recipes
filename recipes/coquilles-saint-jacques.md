## Ingredients

### Potato Purée:
- 340 g (2 cups) Russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) butter
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) 35% cream

### Scallop Filling:
- 2 French shallots, finely minced
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) butter
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) unbleached all-purpose flour
- 125 ml (1/2 cup) milk
- 60 ml (1/4 cup) white wine
- 300 g (11 oz) medium scallops (15-25), drained and dried
- 100 g (1 cup) grated Gruyère cheese

## Instructions

### Potato Purée:
1. Boil cubed potatoes in salted water for 20 minutes until tender and drain.
2. Mash with butter, then beat with electric mixer into smooth purée, adding cream as needed. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to piping bag with large ridged tip.
3. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).

### Scallop Filling:
1. Sauté shallots in butter, add flour and cook 1 minute.
2. Add milk and wine, stirring constantly. Simmer 1 minute. Remove from heat, season, then fold in scallops and half the cheese.
3. Divide mixture among four shells. Pipe potato purée around edges. Top with remaining cheese.
4. Bake 10 minutes, then broil until golden.
