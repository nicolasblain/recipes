## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups brown jasmine rice
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil or olive oil
- 1 small white onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, finely grated
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 yellow/orange/green bell pepper, sliced
- 3 carrots, sliced diagonally
- 2 tablespoons Thai red curry paste
- 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
- ½ cup water
- 1½ cups packed kale, thinly sliced
- 1½ teaspoons coconut sugar
- 1 tablespoon tamari or soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons rice vinegar or lime juice
- Fresh basil or cilantro for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook rinsed jasmine rice in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and let it rest covered for 10 minutes.

2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion with a pinch of salt and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the ginger and garlic until fragrant.

3. Add the sliced bell peppers and carrots to the pan, cooking until they're tender, approximately 3-5 minutes. Add the curry paste and cook for another 2 minutes, stirring frequently.

4. Pour in the coconut milk, water, kale, and coconut sugar. Simmer until all vegetables are soft, about 5-10 minutes.

5. Remove from heat and season with tamari and rice vinegar. Taste and adjust salt as needed. Serve over the cooked rice and garnish with fresh basil or cilantro if desired.

## Notes

A versatile curry that works well with additional vegetables or protein.
