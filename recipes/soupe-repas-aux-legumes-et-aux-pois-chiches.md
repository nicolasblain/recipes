## Ingredients

- 1/2 rutabaga, diced
- 1 carrot, sliced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 ml cumin
- 1 ml coriander
- 1 ml turmeric
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- 45 ml olive oil
- 2 cans (540 ml each) chickpeas, rinsed
- 1.5 liters vegetable broth
- 15 ml tomato paste
- 5 ml harissa paste
- 15 g flat parsley, chopped
- 15 g fresh mint, chopped
- 60 ml plain yogurt
- 35 g Corinth raisins
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Brown rutabaga, carrot, onion, 2 garlic cloves, and spices in 30 ml oil over medium heat. Add half the chickpeas, broth, tomato paste, and harissa. Season with salt and pepper, bring to a boil, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes until vegetables are tender.

2. Reserve 2 cups of mixture. Blend remaining mixture until smooth, strain, return to pot, and add remaining chickpeas. Keep warm.

3. Sauté remaining garlic in remaining oil over medium heat. Add herbs briefly until just wilted.

4. Distribute soup among bowls. Top with reserved vegetables, yogurt, herbs, and raisins.
