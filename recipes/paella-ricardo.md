## Ingredients

- 340 g chicken thighs
- 340 g shrimp
- Squid and mussels (about 20)
- 150 g chorizo sausage, sliced
- 2 artichoke hearts
- 330 g paella rice
- 1 liter chicken broth
- 125 ml white wine
- Olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 15 ml tomato paste
- 5 ml sweet paprika
- Saffron strands
- 125 ml peas
- Lemon wedges for serving

## Instructions

1. Prepare a warm broth by combining bouillon, tomato paste, paprika, and artichoke hearts. Add saffron once boiling.
2. Brown chicken in oil, then add onion and garlic. Incorporate pepper, squid, and chorizo before adding rice.
3. Deglaze with white wine, then add the prepared broth and simmer for 10 minutes without stirring.
4. Arrange shrimp on top and nestle mussels into rice. Cook 10-12 minutes until rice is tender and seafood is cooked.
5. Remove from heat, stir in peas, cover loosely with foil, and rest 3 minutes before serving with lemon wedges.
