## Ingredients

- 8 slices pancetta
- 454 g medium shrimp, peeled and deveined, tailless
- 5 ml sweet paprika
- 60 ml olive oil
- 2 cans (398 ml each) white beans, rinsed and drained
- 500 ml cherry tomatoes, halved
- 30 ml fresh basil leaves, torn
- 30 ml fresh chives, chopped
- 45 ml red wine vinegar
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- Espelette pepper, to taste
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Position oven rack in center and preheat to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. Arrange pancetta on the sheet and bake for about 12 minutes until crispy.

3. In a bowl, combine shrimp with paprika, 30 ml oil, salt, and pepper.

4. Grill shrimp in a grill pan for 2-3 minutes per side until cooked through. Set aside.

5. In another bowl, combine white beans, tomatoes, basil, chives, vinegar, garlic, Espelette pepper, remaining oil, salt, and pepper.

6. Break cooled pancetta into pieces and fold into the bean mixture along with the warm shrimp. Serve immediately.
