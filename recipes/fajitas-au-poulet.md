## Ingredients

- 2 red or yellow peppers, sliced
- 60 ml olive oil
- 2-3 onions, minced
- 15 ml butter
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
- 15 ml chili seasoning
- 5 ml dried oregano
- 8 medium tortillas
- Salt and pepper
- Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, guacamole, salsa, sour cream, shredded cheddar, lime wedges, fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Preheat a cast-iron fajita skillet to 200°C (400°F), or prepare a serving plate for hot fajitas.

2. Brown peppers in 15 ml oil over medium heat, season, and set aside.

3. Caramelize onions in remaining oil and butter, season, and reserve with peppers.

4. Brown chicken in remaining oil with spices. Return vegetables to pan and cook for 1 minute while stirring. Adjust seasoning as needed.

5. Remove from heat, transfer mixture to the hot skillet, and serve immediately with toppings in separate bowls.
