## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters finely chopped cabbage (about 1 small head, 1 kg)
- 250 ml finely chopped celery
- 60 ml finely chopped onion
- 125 ml relish
- 180 ml mayonnaise
- 60 ml milk
- 30 ml white vinegar
- 30 ml sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients.

2. Season with salt and pepper.

3. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving to allow all flavors to blend well.

## Notes

This coleslaw keeps 2-3 days refrigerated in an airtight container.
