## Ingredients

- 2 cans tuna (198 g each) in oil, drained
- 75 ml mayonnaise
- 40 g red onion, finely diced
- 35 g celery, diced small
- 15 ml sweet pickles, diced small
- 2.5 ml curry powder
- 20 ml honey
- 4 large wheat tortillas, 25 cm diameter
- 50 g Boston lettuce leaves

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the tuna with mayonnaise, onion, celery, pickles, and curry powder. Season with pepper.

2. Spread honey over half of each tortilla. Layer lettuce, then tuna mixture on top.

3. Fold the tortilla edges toward the center and roll tightly.

4. Wrap individually in parchment paper, twisting the ends. Cut in half. Serve immediately or refrigerate in plastic wrap until ready to eat.
