## Ingredients

- 1 aubergine, cubed
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 90 ml olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves, finely minced
- 225 g white mushrooms, quartered
- 1 ml crushed hot pepper flakes
- 2 yellow peppers, seeded and cubed
- 2 zucchini, cubed
- 1 can (796 ml) diced tomatoes, drained
- 4 fresh thyme sprigs
- 10 g chopped basil
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat half the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Brown eggplant and onion, season with salt and pepper. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute, then transfer to a bowl.

2. In the same pot, brown mushrooms with pepper flakes in remaining oil. Combine with eggplant mixture.

3. Brown peppers and zucchini, adding more oil if needed. Return eggplant mixture to pot.

4. Add tomatoes and thyme, mix well. Bring to a boil and simmer gently for 15-20 minutes.

5. Remove thyme branches, stir in basil, and adjust seasoning. Serve with pasta, grilled fish, chicken, or on pizza.
