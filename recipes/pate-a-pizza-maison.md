## Ingredients

- 250 ml (1 cup) warm water
- 5 ml (1 tsp) instant yeast
- 5 ml (1 tsp) sugar
- 300 g (2 cups) unbleached all-purpose flour
- 5 ml (1 tsp) salt

## Instructions

1. Combine water, yeast, and sugar in a bowl and let rest until foamy on top, about 5 minutes.
2. Using a food processor with plastic blade or dough hook, mix flour and salt, then gradually add yeast mixture at medium speed until a soft ball forms.
3. Hand-knead dough on lightly floured surface for a few minutes, form into a ball, place in oiled bowl, cover with clean cloth and let rise 30 minutes in a warm, humid location.
4. Divide dough in half and use immediately, refrigerate (under 48 hours), or freeze.

## Notes

Makes enough for two 20 cm (8 inch) diameter pizzas with generous crust, or two 23 cm (9 inch) thin crust pizzas.
