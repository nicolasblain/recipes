## Ingredients

- 3 cloves garlic, quartered
- 2 egg whites
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 2.5 ml salt
- 375 ml canola oil
- 45 ml water
- 45 ml heavy cream (35%)
- 30 ml canola oil
- 15 ml lemon juice
- 1 ml Lebanese 7-spice blend
- 1 ml dried thyme
- 2.5 ml salt
- 675 g boneless, skinless chicken breasts

## Instructions

1. Combine garlic, egg whites, lemon juice, and salt in a tall container. Using an immersion blender, puree ingredients. With blender running, add oil in a thin stream until emulsified. Alternate adding water with remaining oil. Reserve 75 ml for marinade and refrigerate the rest.

2. Whisk reserved sauce with cream, oil, lemon juice, spices, and salt. Coat chicken and refrigerate for 8-12 hours.

3. Preheat barbecue to medium-high heat and oil grill.

4. Cook chicken for 7-8 minutes per side until internal temperature reaches 74°C (165°F).

5. Rest for 5 minutes, then slice. Serve with garlic sauce and green salad or rice.
