## Ingredients

- 30 ml hoisin sauce
- 15 ml reduced-sodium soy sauce
- 5 ml rice vinegar
- 5 ml toasted sesame oil
- 450 g salmon fillet (skinless), cut into 12 pieces
- 15 ml vegetable oil
- 3 packages (120 g each) dried ramen noodles
- 150 g frozen peas
- 45 ml toasted sesame oil
- 45 ml reduced-sodium soy sauce
- 15 ml rice vinegar
- 2.5 ml sambal oelek
- 2 celery stalks, thinly sliced
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 45 ml fresh mint leaves, torn
- 45 ml fresh cilantro leaves, torn
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Whisk together 30 ml hoisin, 15 ml soy sauce, 5 ml vinegar, and 5 ml sesame oil. Coat salmon pieces in this marinade.

2. Sear the salmon in vegetable oil over medium heat for 2 minutes per side until lightly cooked.

3. Cook the ramen noodles and peas in salted boiling water for 2-3 minutes until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.

4. Whisk together 45 ml sesame oil, 45 ml soy sauce, 15 ml vinegar, and sambal oelek. Season with pepper.

5. Toss the noodles and peas with the dressing and celery, green onions, mint, and cilantro.

6. Divide into bowls, top with salmon, and garnish with sesame seeds.
