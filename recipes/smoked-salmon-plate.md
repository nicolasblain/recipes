## Ingredients

- 80–100g smoked salmon
- ½ cucumber, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp cream cheese
- 1 tbsp capers
- Squeeze of lemon
- Fresh dill (optional)
- Black pepper

## Instructions

1. Arrange the smoked salmon on a plate.
2. Fan out the cucumber slices alongside.
3. Add dollops of cream cheese.
4. Scatter the capers over everything.
5. Squeeze lemon over the salmon, crack some black pepper, add dill if using. Serve.

## Notes

- This is the no-bagel version of a classic. The cucumber replaces the bread and adds crunch and volume.
- Full-fat cream cheese, not the low-fat kind.
- Works well with a soft-boiled egg on the side if you want more protein.
