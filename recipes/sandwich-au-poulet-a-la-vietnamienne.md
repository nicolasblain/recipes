## Ingredients

- 60 ml mayonnaise
- 15 ml mirin
- 10 ml hoisin sauce
- 10 ml chili sauce (sambal oelek, sriracha, etc.) or to taste
- 0.5 ml five-spice powder
- 1 baguette
- 500 ml cooked chicken, packed and torn into chunks
- 1 Lebanese cucumber, cut into thin julienne
- 1 carrot, cut into thin julienne
- 1 green onion, sliced
- Fresh cilantro leaves to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, mirin, hoisin sauce, chili sauce, and five-spice powder in a large bowl.

2. Split the baguette horizontally without fully separating the halves. Spread half the mayonnaise mixture inside.

3. Cut the baguette into 3-4 sections.

4. Toss the chicken with the remaining mayonnaise until well coated.

5. Distribute the chicken on the bread base. Top with cucumber, carrot, green onion, and cilantro.
