## Ingredients

- 450 g asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2.5 cm pieces
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 30 ml olive oil
- 15 ml mayonnaise
- 15 ml Dijon mustard
- 15 ml lemon juice
- 1 can (540 ml) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 1 zucchini, cut into small dice
- 2 blocks (200 g each) halloumi cheese, cut into 6 thick slices each
- 25 g fresh basil leaves, roughly torn

## Instructions

1. In a non-stick skillet over high heat, soften the asparagus and garlic in half the oil until tender-crisp. Season with salt and pepper.

2. Whisk together mayonnaise, mustard, and lemon juice in a bowl. Add the warm asparagus, chickpeas, and zucchini. Season to taste and set aside.

3. Pat the cheese slices dry with paper towels.

4. In the same skillet over medium-high heat, brown the cheese briefly on each side using the remaining oil.

5. Divide the salad among bowls, top with the grilled cheese slices, and garnish with fresh basil.
