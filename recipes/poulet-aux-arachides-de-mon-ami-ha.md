## Ingredients

- 375 g boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
- 375 g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced into strips
- 5 ml fresh lemongrass, finely minced, or lime zest
- 45 ml hoisin sauce
- 15 ml rice vinegar
- 125 ml coconut milk
- 250 ml chicken broth
- 1 onion, cut into small wedges
- 150 ml crunchy peanut butter
- 1 to 2.5 ml cayenne pepper
- 1 zucchini, halved lengthwise and sliced diagonally
- 2 red peppers, sliced into strips
- 20 thin Chinese green beans or regular green beans, halved lengthwise and blanched
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken, lemongrass, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar in a bowl. Marinate for 20 minutes at room temperature.

2. Bring coconut milk, broth, and onion to a boil in a saucepan.

3. Add peanut butter and cayenne pepper, stirring until the sauce is smooth.

4. Add the marinated chicken and simmer over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

5. Add vegetables and cook for an additional 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Serve over rice or rice vermicelli.
