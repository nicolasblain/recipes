## Ingredients

- 340 g flat rice noodles
- 340 g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced
- 15 ml fish sauce
- 5 ml sweet paprika
- 5 ml brown sugar
- 2 shallots, chopped
- 75 ml canola oil
- 1 leek, sliced
- 4 eggs, lightly beaten
- 340 g firm tofu, cubed and drained
- 250 ml water
- 45 ml lime juice
- 45 ml brown sugar
- 45 ml fish sauce
- 15 ml Sriracha sauce
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 45 ml canola oil
- 30 ml sweet paprika
- 150 g bean sprouts
- 50 g unsalted roasted peanuts, finely ground
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Hydrate rice noodles in warm water for 15-25 minutes.

2. Prepare the sauce by whisking together water, lime juice, brown sugar, fish sauce, Sriracha, garlic, oil, and paprika.

3. Cook chicken with first batch of fish sauce, paprika, and brown sugar in oil until done. Remove and set aside.

4. In the same pot, lightly scramble eggs and remove.

5. Brown tofu and remove.

6. Add leek and cook briefly. Add drained noodles and sauce, then combine with chicken, eggs, and tofu. Stir-fry briefly to warm through.

7. Serve garnished with bean sprouts, peanuts, and lime wedges.
