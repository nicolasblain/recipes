## Ingredients

### Cake:
- 300 g (2 cups) unbleached all-purpose flour
- 5 ml (1 tsp) baking soda
- 5 ml (1 tsp) baking powder
- 2.5 ml (1/2 tsp) salt
- 2 eggs
- 420 g (2 cups) sugar
- 250 ml (1 cup) buttermilk
- 125 ml (1/2 cup) canola oil
- 10 ml (2 tsp) vanilla extract
- 75 g (3/4 cup) cocoa powder
- 180 ml (3/4 cup) strong coffee, lukewarm

### Ganache:
- 450 g (16 oz) dark chocolate, chopped
- 430 ml (1 3/4 cups) heavy cream (35%)
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) light corn syrup

## Instructions

### Cake:
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) with rack in center. Butter two 20 cm (8-inch) springform pans and line with parchment paper.
2. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a bowl and set aside.
3. In another bowl, whisk together eggs, sugar, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla and set aside.
4. Mix cocoa and coffee in a small bowl. Add to egg mixture, then incorporate dry ingredients until smooth. Divide batter between pans.
5. Bake 45-50 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool completely on a rack and unmold.

### Ganache:
1. Place chopped chocolate in a bowl.
2. Heat cream and corn syrup until boiling. Pour over chocolate and wait 2 minutes before stirring.
3. Whisk until smooth and glossy. Refrigerate 1 hour, stirring occasionally, until thickened and workable.

### Assembly:
Level cake tops. Split each cake horizontally into two layers (four total). Spread ganache between layers and stack. Serve on a platter.
