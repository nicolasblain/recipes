## Ingredients

- 15 ml (1 tablespoon) all-purpose flour
- 5 ml (1 teaspoon) salt
- 5 ml (1 teaspoon) ground black pepper
- 5 ml (1 teaspoon) onion powder
- 2.5 ml (1/2 teaspoon) garlic powder
- 1 beef rib roast, approximately 2 kg (4.5 lb) with bone, at room temperature

## Instructions

1. Position oven rack in center and preheat to 260°C (500°F). Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
2. Combine all seasonings in a bowl.
3. Rub the roast thoroughly on all sides with the seasoning mixture.
4. Place roast on prepared sheet, fat-side up. Roast 25 minutes.
5. Turn off oven and keep door closed for 2 hours without opening.
6. Remove roast from oven, slice and serve with béarnaise sauce.

## Notes

Crucial: Keep oven door closed for the entire 2-hour resting period.
