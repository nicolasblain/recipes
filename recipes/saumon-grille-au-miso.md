## Ingredients

- 1 lime, washed
- 1 French shallot, minced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 15 ml fresh ginger, minced
- 45 ml olive oil
- 45 ml unsalted roasted peanuts, chopped
- 30 ml honey
- 2.5 ml sambal oelek
- 675 g salmon fillet with skin
- 15 ml miso
- 15 ml honey
- 30 ml olive oil
- 1 broccoli, stem peeled and quartered, steamed
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Finely zest the lime into a bowl. Peel away white pith and finely chop flesh, adding to zest.

2. In a small pot over medium-high heat, sauté shallot, garlic, and ginger in oil. Add peanuts and honey, cooking for 1 minute. Transfer to lime mixture and stir in sambal oelek. Set aside at room temperature.

3. Combine miso and honey, then brush onto salmon. Oil salmon and broccoli, seasoning with salt and pepper.

4. Preheat barbecue to high and oil grate. Grill broccoli on both sides and keep warm.

5. Grill salmon skin-side up for 2-3 minutes until marked. Flip carefully and cook for about 10 minutes to desired doneness.

6. Plate salmon, arrange broccoli around it, drizzle with condiment, and serve extra on the side.
