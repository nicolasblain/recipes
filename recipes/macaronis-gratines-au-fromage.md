## Ingredients

- 340 g short macaroni pasta
- 30 ml butter
- 30 ml all-purpose unbleached flour
- 2.5 ml dry mustard
- 1 ml onion powder
- 1 ml garlic powder
- 1 can (354 ml) evaporated milk
- 250 ml chicken broth
- 300 g shredded strong orange cheddar
- 200 g shredded havarti cheese
- 110 g Velveeta, cubed
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente and drain.

2. Preheat broiler and butter a 2-liter baking dish or four 500-ml ramekins.

3. Melt butter on medium heat. Whisk in flour and spices, cooking for 1 minute. Pour in evaporated milk and broth, bring to a boil.

4. Remove from heat and stir in half the cheddar, half the havarti, and all the Velveeta until melted. Fold in pasta and reheat for 2 minutes. Adjust seasonings.

5. Transfer to baking dish, top with remaining cheeses, and broil for 5 minutes until lightly golden.
