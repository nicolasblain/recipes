## Ingredients

- 1.1 kg sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 115 g pancetta, diced
- 5 ml sweet paprika
- 360 g kale, stems removed, leaves torn
- 15 ml olive oil
- 215 g cooked chicken, shredded
- 25 g roasted pecans
- 45 ml mayonnaise
- 15 ml white wine vinegar
- 15 ml Dijon mustard
- 5 ml maple syrup
- 1 shallot, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F) with rack centered. Line a baking sheet with a silicone mat.

2. Combine sweet potatoes, pancetta, and paprika on the sheet. Season with salt and pepper. Bake for 20 minutes until tender and golden, stirring halfway through.

3. Toss kale with olive oil and season. Add kale and chicken to the baking sheet. Bake for an additional 5 minutes. Adjust seasoning.

4. Whisk together mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, and maple syrup. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in the sliced shallot.

5. Divide the salad among bowls. Top with pecans and drizzle with the vinaigrette before serving.
