## Ingredients

- 340 g cooked chicken, diced
- 2 celery branches, chopped
- 4 green onions, finely chopped
- 90 ml mayonnaise
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 100 g crumbled feta cheese (optional)
- Salt and pepper
- 1 baguette, halved horizontally
- 40 g baby spinach
- 10 g celery leaves
- 15 ml olive oil
- 4 long sandwich picks

## Instructions

1. Combine the chicken, celery, green onions, mayonnaise, and lemon juice in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in the feta cheese if using.

2. Line the bread base with the chicken salad.

3. Distribute the spinach and celery leaves over the salad.

4. Drizzle with olive oil.

5. Close with the top half of the bread.

6. Secure with four sandwich picks and cut into four portions.

## Notes

Approximately 550 calories per serving.
