## Ingredients

- 450 g sweet potatoes, unpeeled, washed, sliced into 1 cm half-rounds
- 1 can (540 ml) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 30 ml olive oil
- 15 g fresh dill, chopped, plus extra for serving
- 45 ml olive oil
- 1 lime, zest finely grated and juice
- 1 small garlic clove, minced
- 5 ml honey
- 150 g halloumi cheese, cut into two long slices
- 2 Lebanese cucumbers, sliced into rounds
- 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 230°C (450°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. Toss sweet potatoes, chickpeas, and 30 ml oil on the sheet. Season with salt and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes until tender and golden, stirring halfway through.

3. Meanwhile, whisk together dill, 45 ml oil, lime zest and juice, garlic, and honey in a large bowl. Season to taste.

4. Sear halloumi in a non-stick pan over high heat for 1 minute per side. Cut into large cubes.

5. Add cucumbers, onion, and roasted vegetables to the vinaigrette bowl. Toss well and adjust seasoning.

6. Divide salad between bowls, top with halloumi cubes, and garnish with fresh dill.
