## Ingredients

- 250 ml (1 cup) sugar
- 180 ml (3/4 cup) hot espresso or strong coffee
- 60 ml (1/4 cup) milk
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) coffee liqueur (Tia Maria or similar)
- 5 egg yolks
- 2.5 ml (1/2 tsp) vanilla extract
- 475-500 g (17-18 oz) mascarpone cheese, at room temperature
- 375 ml (1 1/2 cups) heavy cream (35%)
- 30 ladyfinger biscuits
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) cocoa powder, sifted

## Instructions

1. Combine 30 ml sugar with hot coffee. Add milk and liqueur. Chill this mixture.
2. Whisk egg yolks, vanilla, and 60 ml sugar over a double boiler until ribbon-like (approximately 5 minutes). Continue whisking with an electric mixer until lukewarm. Fold in mascarpone.
3. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream with remaining sugar (150 ml) until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into the mascarpone mixture.
4. Dip ladyfingers briefly (2-3 seconds) into the coffee mixture and layer in a 28 x 20 cm dish. Spread half the mascarpone filling and dust with half the cocoa. Repeat layers and finish with cocoa.
5. Cover and refrigerate minimum 4 hours or overnight.

## Notes

Requires 24 hours minimum chilling time (though 4 hours is acceptable minimum).
