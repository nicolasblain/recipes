## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas (about 4-5 bananas)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
4. Stir the banana mixture into the flour mixture just until moistened. Do not overmix.
5. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. Let bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

## Notes

- The riper the bananas, the sweeter and more flavorful the bread. Black-spotted bananas are ideal.
- You can fold in 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips if desired.
- Freeze overripe bananas (peeled) so you always have them ready for banana bread.
- Stores well wrapped at room temperature for 2-3 days, or freeze slices for up to 3 months.
