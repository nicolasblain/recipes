## Ingredients

- 40 g panko breadcrumbs
- 35 g freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 225 g white mushrooms, quartered
- 55 g butter
- 30 ml all-purpose flour
- 310 ml chicken broth
- 250 ml milk
- 1 can (284 ml) condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 2 cans (198 g each) tuna in oil, drained
- 225 g frozen peas, thawed
- 1 lemon, zest finely grated
- 340 g large spiral egg noodles
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Position oven rack at center and preheat to 220°C (425°F).

2. Combine breadcrumbs with Parmesan in a bowl and set aside.

3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, sauté mushrooms in butter for 5 minutes. Dust with flour and cook for 1 minute while stirring. Add broth, milk, and cream soup, bring to a boil while whisking until thickened, then simmer for 2 minutes.

4. Remove from heat and stir in tuna, peas, and lemon zest. Season with salt and pepper.

5. Cook noodles al dente in salted boiling water and drain. Toss with sauce. Transfer to a 33 x 23 cm baking dish, top with breadcrumb mixture, and bake for 17-20 minutes until golden. Rest for 5 minutes before serving.
