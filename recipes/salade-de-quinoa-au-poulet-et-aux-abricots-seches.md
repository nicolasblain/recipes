## Ingredients

- 95 g quinoa, rinsed and drained
- 45 ml mayonnaise
- 1 French shallot, minced
- 1 lemon, zest finely grated
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 255 g cooked chicken, diced
- 220 g grated carrots
- 115 g gouda cheese, diced
- 100 g dried apricots, finely diced
- 50 g roasted pecans, crushed
- 15 g fresh basil, roughly torn
- 10 g flat parsley, leaves only
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook rinsed quinoa in salted boiling water for approximately 15 minutes until tender. Cool under cold running water and drain.

2. In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, shallot, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.

3. Add the cooked quinoa and all remaining ingredients. Mix well and adjust seasoning as needed.

4. Divide the salad among bowls and serve immediately.
