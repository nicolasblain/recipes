## Ingredients

- 10 ml crushed mustard seeds
- 5 ml crushed coriander seeds
- 5 ml crushed black pepper
- 1 ml ground cinnamon
- 1 beef roast (approximately 1.4 kg/3 lb) of tenderloin or top sirloin
- 30 ml Dijon mustard
- 4 sliced onions
- 60 ml butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

### For the sauce:
- 30 ml cornstarch
- 30 ml water
- 2 liters chicken broth
- 500 ml Marsala wine or Pineau des Charentes
- 30 ml molasses
- 125 ml heavy cream (35%)
- 60 ml unsalted butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F) with rack in center.
2. Mix spices in a bowl, coat roast with Dijon mustard then spice mixture.
3. Sear roast in half the butter in an oven-safe skillet, season and set aside.
4. Sauté onions in remaining butter, return roast to pan and bake approximately 45 minutes until meat thermometer reads 48°C (118°F) for rare. Rest 10 minutes.

### For the sauce:
1. Whisk cornstarch with water.
2. Reduce broth, wine, and molasses to 1 liter. Add cornstarch mixture and cream, and boil while stirring.
3. Stir in butter until melted and season to taste.
4. Slice meat thinly, drizzle with sauce and serve with onions and vegetables.
