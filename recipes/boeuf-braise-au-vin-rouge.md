## Ingredients

- 1 boneless beef chuck roast (approximately 1.5 kg/3 lb)
- 60 ml olive oil
- 3 French shallots, chopped
- 10 ml crushed juniper berries
- 2.5 ml crushed black pepper
- 1 ml ground nutmeg
- 30 ml all-purpose flour
- 500 ml red wine
- 500 ml veal stock or beef broth (heated)
- 15 ml ketchup
- 4 large carrots, diced
- 115 g pancetta, diced
- 15 ml honey
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) with rack in center.
2. Brown meat in half the oil in a pot, season both sides and set aside. Soften shallots and spices in remaining oil, add flour and mix. Deglaze with wine and bring to boil while whisking.
3. Return meat to pot with half the stock and ketchup. Bring to boil, cover and braise for 3 hours, turning meat 3-4 times and basting with remaining stock.
4. Add carrots and pancetta, continue covered cooking for about 1 hour until meat shreds easily.
5. Place oven rack high and switch to broil. Drain meat onto baking sheet, brush with honey and broil for approximately 3 minutes until surface caramelizes. Serve with vegetables and sauce.
