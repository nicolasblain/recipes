## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Powdered sugar, for serving
- Fresh lemon juice, for serving
- Fresh berries or sliced fruit (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the butter in a 9x13-inch baking dish and put it in the oven to melt while preheating.
2. In a blender or large bowl, beat the eggs until light and frothy. Gradually add the flour, milk, and salt, blending until smooth.
3. Remove the hot baking dish from the oven. Swirl the melted butter to coat the bottom and sides of the dish.
4. Pour the batter into the hot, buttered dish.
5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until the pancake is puffed up and golden brown. It will puff dramatically in the oven and deflate slightly when removed.
6. Serve immediately, dusted with powdered sugar and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

## Notes

- The pancake will puff up spectacularly in the oven -- serve it right away for the best presentation.
- A cast iron skillet works great instead of a baking dish and gives extra-crispy edges.
- Top with fresh fruit, maple syrup, jam, or whipped cream.
- Make sure the butter and pan are very hot before adding the batter for the best rise.
