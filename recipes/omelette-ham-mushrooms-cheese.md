## Ingredients

- 3 large eggs
- 50g cooked ham, diced
- 80g mushrooms, sliced
- 30g cheddar or gruyère, grated
- 1 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper in a bowl.
2. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for 3–4 minutes until softened and any released moisture has evaporated. Add the ham and toss for 1 minute. Remove from pan and set aside.
3. In the same pan over medium-low heat, pour in the beaten eggs. Let the edges set, then gently lift them and tilt the pan to let uncooked egg flow underneath.
4. When the top is almost set but still slightly glossy, add the ham and mushroom filling to one half. Top with cheese.
5. Fold the omelette over the filling and slide onto a plate. Serve immediately.

## Notes

- Don't brown the omelette — keep the heat low for a soft, pale result.
- Any firm melting cheese works: cheddar, gruyère, comté, gouda.
- Deli ham is fine; leftover roast ham or prosciutto is even better.
