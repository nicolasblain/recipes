## Ingredients

- 2 French shallots, minced
- 125 ml olive oil
- 30 ml piri piri sauce
- 30 ml sweet paprika
- 125 ml white wine
- 125 ml chicken broth
- 115 g softened unsalted butter
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 whole chicken (approximately 1.8 kg)
- 20 ml coarse salt
- 1/2 lemon

## Instructions

1. Sauté shallots in 60 ml oil, add piri piri and paprika, cooking for 1 minute. Add remaining oil, wine, broth, and butter, then simmer for approximately 15 minutes until thickened. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.

2. Remove chicken backbone and flatten. Rub salt and lemon inside and outside for 3 minutes, then rest for 5 minutes.

3. Coat chicken with 60 ml sauce in a glass dish. Cover and refrigerate for 12 or more hours. Store remaining sauce separately.

4. Heat half the barbecue on high. Place chicken skin-side down on the unlit side. Cover and cook for 45 minutes. Flip and cook for approximately 30 minutes until internal temperature reaches 82°C (180°F). Finish by searing skin-side over heat.

5. Reheat reserved sauce, cut chicken into pieces, and brush generously with sauce.
