## Ingredients

- 2 small pepper squashes
- 30 ml olive oil
- 10 ml honey
- 125 ml quinoa, rinsed and drained
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 30 ml olive oil
- 5 ml harissa
- 1 roasted red bell pepper, peeled and diced
- 125 ml roasted peanuts, crushed
- 60 ml raisins
- 180 ml shredded cheddar cheese
- 30 ml fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. Halve squashes lengthwise and remove seeds. Oil, salt, and pepper them, then place cut-side down on sheet and bake for 40-45 minutes until tender. Brush interiors with honey.

3. Cook quinoa in salted boiling water for 12-15 minutes until tender, then rinse and drain.

4. Sauté onion and garlic in oil. Stir in harissa, then add quinoa, pepper, half the peanuts, and raisins, warming through. Mix in half the cheese and cilantro, and season to taste.

5. Distribute filling into squash halves, top with remaining cheese and peanuts, and bake for 10 minutes until cheese melts.
