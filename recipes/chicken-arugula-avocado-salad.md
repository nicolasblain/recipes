## Ingredients

- 1–2 chicken thighs, bone-in or boneless
- 2 large handfuls arugula
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 100g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of ½ lemon
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Season chicken thighs generously with salt and pepper. Grill or cook in a cast iron pan over medium-high heat — about 6–7 minutes per side for boneless, longer for bone-in, until cooked through with a good sear.
2. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice.
3. Toss the arugula and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Arrange the avocado slices over the salad.
5. Top with the sliced chicken and serve.

## Notes

- Thighs over breasts — they stay juicier and have more flavour, especially when grilled.
- Don't dress the salad until just before serving or it will wilt.
- A spoonful of Dijon mustard whisked into the dressing adds depth.
- No croutons, no sugary dressings.
