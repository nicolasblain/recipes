## Ingredients

### Vegetables:
- 800 g white potatoes, sliced thin
- 90 ml olive oil
- 2 medium eggplants, cut into 1 cm rounds
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 7.5 ml dried oregano

### Meat Filling:
- 675 g ground lamb or lean beef
- 15 ml olive oil
- 1 ml ground cinnamon
- Pinch of cayenne pepper
- 2 onions, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 15 ml tomato paste
- 10 ml sweet paprika
- 125 ml dry white wine
- 1 can (796 ml) whole Italian tomatoes

### Béchamel:
- 85 g butter
- 75 g all-purpose flour
- 625 ml milk
- 0.5 ml ground nutmeg
- 35 g grated pecorino romano cheese
- 2 egg yolks

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Roast potatoes and eggplants until tender.
3. Brown ground meat with spices in a pot. Drain excess fat.
4. Sauté onions and garlic, then add tomato paste, paprika, wine, and tomatoes. Simmer until thickened.
5. Prepare béchamel: Make a roux with butter and flour, add milk gradually, season with nutmeg. Remove from heat and incorporate cheese and egg yolks.
6. In a baking dish, layer roasted vegetables, then meat sauce, then béchamel.
7. Bake until golden. Rest 15 minutes before serving.
