## Ingredients

- 75 g (1/2 cup) unbleached all-purpose flour
- 1 ml (1/4 tsp) salt
- 2 eggs
- 250 ml (1 cup) chocolate-hazelnut spread
- 105 g (1/2 cup) brown sugar
- 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
- 115 g (1/2 cup) unsalted butter, melted and cooled

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 165°C (325°F). Butter a square 20 cm (8-inch) pan and line with parchment paper with overhang.
2. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, chocolate-hazelnut spread, brown sugar, and vanilla for 2 minutes until smooth. At low speed, alternate incorporating dry ingredients with melted butter. Spread batter in pan.
4. Bake 35-40 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center emerges with crumbs (not clean).
5. Cool in pan for approximately 2 hours.
6. Unmold and cut into 16 squares. Store up to 5 days in an airtight container at room temperature. Serve warm or cold.
