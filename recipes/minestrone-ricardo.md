## Ingredients

- 60 ml sweet pancetta, diced
- 375 ml leek, white part only, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
- 45 ml olive oil
- 180 ml celery, diced
- 250 ml carrots, diced
- 250 ml green beans, cut into 1 cm pieces
- 1.75 liters chicken broth
- 1 bay leaf
- 125 ml canned white beans, rinsed and drained
- 250 ml zucchini, diced
- 60 ml short pasta (such as tubetti)
- 5 Italian tomatoes, grilled, peeled, and chopped
- 60 ml fresh basil, chopped
- 15 ml fresh oregano, chopped
- 125 ml Parmigiano Reggiano shavings (for garnish)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Brown pancetta, leek, and garlic in oil in a pot. Add celery, carrots, and green beans, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 3 minutes.

2. Add broth and bay leaf. Cover, bring to a boil, and simmer for 10 minutes. Add white beans, zucchini, and pasta.

3. Continue cooking for approximately 10 minutes until pasta reaches al dente texture.

4. Stir in tomatoes, basil, and oregano. Adjust seasonings as needed.

5. Pour into bowls and top with Parmigiano Reggiano shavings.
