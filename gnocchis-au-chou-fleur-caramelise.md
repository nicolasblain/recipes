## Ingredients

- 450 g store-bought gnocchi
- 1 cauliflower, cut into large florets, then sliced 1 cm thick
- 55 g butter
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 250 ml vegetable broth
- 10 g chopped chives
- 35 g grated fresh Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook gnocchi in salted boiling water until they float to the surface. Drain, spread on a plate, and lightly oil.

2. In a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat, cook cauliflower in butter until well browned, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Pour in broth and reduce by half.

4. Add gnocchi and chives, warming for 2 minutes while stirring gently. Adjust seasoning and top with Parmesan.

5. Serve with grilled pork or chicken escalope if desired.
